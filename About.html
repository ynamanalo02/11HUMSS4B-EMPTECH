About Us

About Far Eastern University: Shaping Futures Through Quality Education

Founded as the Institute of Accountancy, Far Eastern University (FEU) has a proud legacy of excellence in education, initially offering a three-year program designed to meet the rigorous standards for the Certified Public Accountant examination. Under the visionary leadership of its first president, Dr. Nicanor Reyes, FEU aimed to be a beacon for aspiring accountants in the Philippines.

Over the years, FEU has undergone significant expansion, enhancing its facilities to accommodate a growing student body and adapting to the evolving demands of modern education. In response to the needs of a developing nation, the university established the Institute of Graduate Studies to elevate academic excellence through advanced instruction and professional training.

Today, FEU is recognized as one of the leading universities in the Philippines, celebrated for its rich history in education, culture, arts, and sports. Our commitment to providing a well-rounded education is reflected in our diverse programs and innovative approaches that empower students to thrive. With a strong focus on faculty development, research initiatives, and state-of-the-art facilities, we continue to ascend toward higher standards of quality education.

At FEU, we are dedicated to nurturing independent thinkers and globally competitive professionals, preparing them to contribute meaningfully to society while upholding Philippine culture and values. Join us in our journey toward excellence, where every student is given the opportunity to shine.

Our Vision

Far Eastern University envisions itself as contributing to the creation of an inclusive and sustainable society that is progressive in outlook, provides equitable opportunities, and upholds Philippine culture, values, and history.

Our Mission

Far Eastern University provides inclusive, learner-centered education that produces graduates who are independent thinkers, highly employable, and globally competitive professionals. We serve as a catalyst for the creation of new, practicable knowledge in solving pressing societal concerns and as an active repository of Philippine art, culture, and heritage, sharing our intellectual resources with a wider community to enhance the economic prospects of the communities we serve.

Our Core Values

The FEU core values are fortitude, excellence, and uprightness. Fortitude refers to the ability to work hard, persevere, not give in or give up; to be committed to an ideal and to pursue it with hard work and courage. The school motto “Be Brave” is a call to fortitude. Excellence refers to the ability to do rigorous, meticulous, and relevant work that is comparable to the highest standards. Uprightness refers to moral and ethical integrity, a willingness to put others ahead of the self, a commitment to fairness, and a commitment to work for the greater good.
