Academics

Courses We Offer

Far Eastern University (FEU) provides a diverse range of undergraduate and graduate programs designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary for success in their chosen fields.

Basic Education (K-12)

Junior High School (Grades 7-10): FEU provides a comprehensive curriculum that focuses on developing foundational skills and knowledge essential for higher education.

Senior High School (Grades 11-12): The Senior High School program offers four specialized tracks:

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM): Prepares students for fields in science and technology, fostering analytical skills and innovation.

Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM): Focuses on business principles and practices, preparing students for careers in finance and management.

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS): Emphasizes understanding human behavior, society, and culture, making it ideal for students interested in social sciences, arts, and communication.

General Academic Strand (GAS): Offers a flexible curriculum that allows students to explore various subjects, helping them decide on their future educational paths.

College

Undergraduate Programs: FEU offers a diverse range of undergraduate programs designed to equip students with the necessary skills for future success. Students can choose from various disciplines, including Business, Engineering, Arts, and Sciences, ensuring a well-rounded education that prepares them for the global workforce.

Graduate

Advanced Studies: FEU's graduate programs enhance knowledge and expertise through specialized studies, allowing students to deepen their understanding of their chosen fields. These programs include Master's degrees and Doctoral programs that focus on research, leadership, and advanced professional skills.
